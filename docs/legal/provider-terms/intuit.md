# Intuit Developer Terms Summary

Official source:

- `https://developer.intuit.com/app/developer/qbo/docs/legal-agreements/intuit-terms-of-service-for-intuit-developer-services`

Summary:

- The Intuit Developer Platform is governed by a binding developer agreement.
- Developers can accept on behalf of the company or client they represent if they are authorized to do so.
- Developer account information and credentials must remain accurate, current, and confidential.
- Intuit can revoke or suspend access if the platform is used outside the allowed terms.
- The agreement includes prohibitions on unauthorized use, scraping, reverse engineering, and
  commercializing the platform outside the permitted scope.
